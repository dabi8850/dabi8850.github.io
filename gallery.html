3 Staple Muscle Making Recipes

Some are sweet and some are savory, but all seven of these recipes are protein-packed and perfect for your healthy lifestyle! Pick one out and let's go make it!
There's no such thing as having too many healthy recipes. Variation and experimentation keep food fun and your diet from growing intolerable. Even if you're a master of your "fit kitchen," these recipes will make an awesome addition to your collection. Pull up a chair and give these delicious recipes a look—and then a taste! I guarantee you'll find more than one that will make you stop everything and run to the nearest grocery store. If you have any questions, hit me up in the comments below. Eat well and hit your fitness goals!

Lean Beef Meat Ball Spinach Pasta

Ingredients



Meatballs
o 6 oz lean ground beef
o 1/2 cup shredded raw spinach
o 1/4 cup diced red onion
o 1 tbsp minced garlic
o 1/2 tbsp cumin
o Sea salt and pepper, to taste

Pasta
• 2 oz wheat spinach pasta
• 1/8 cup marinara (natural and low sodium)
• 1 1/2 cup raw spinach
• 5 cherry tomatoes
• 1 tbsp low fat parmesan cheese

Directions

1. Set oven to 405 degrees F.

2. Saute the red onions in a skillet using spray olive or coconut oil.

3. Mix together ground beef, chopped raw spinach, red onion, garlic, and spices. Mix thoroughly using your hands.

4. Form 2-3 meatballs of roughly the same size using your hands. For accuracy, use a scale to weigh each portion.

5. Place meatballs on a baking sheet and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes.

6. Cook pasta and stir in tomatoes, spinach, and cheese as desired. Mix in meatballs and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 468

Total Fat: 6g

Total Carbs: 50g

Protein: 51g



Mustard Baked Salmon With Grilled Aspargus

Ingredients




Fish
o 5 oz wild salmon (measured raw)


Marinade
• 1 tbsp dijon mustard
• 1/2 tbsp olive oil
• 1 tsp minced garlic
• Juice from half of a lemon
• 1 1/2 cup grilled asparagus
• 1/2 tbsp minced garlic

Directions

1. Set oven to 405 degrees F.

2. Mix mustard, olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice.

3. Pour the marinade over the salmon. For better flavor, place salomon in fridge for 1 hour.

4. Place the salmon on a baking sheet and top with slices of lemon (if desired). Bake for 10-12 minutes.

5. Set a nonstick skillet on medium high heat and lightly spray with coconut or olive oil (if desired).

6. Toss the asparagus and garlic in the skillet and sear for about 5 minutes. Plate asparagus with salmon and eat up!

Nutrition Facts

Calories 282

Total Fat 16g

Total Carbs 7g

Protein 32g



Tomato Feta-Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast With Brown Rice

Ingredients




Chicken
• 6 oz chicken breast
• 1/2 cup raw spinach
• 1 Roma tomato
• 2 tbsp feta cheese
• 1/2 cup brown rice

Directions

1. Set oven to 375 degrees F.

2. Slice the chicken breast down the middle to make it look like a butterfly; be careful not to slice all the way through the chicken breast. Season with seasoning of choice.

3. Open the chicken breast and, on one side, layer spinach, tomato slices, and feta cheese.

4. Fold the chicken breast like a sandwich and use toothpicks to hold the chicken breast closed.

5. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until the chicken breast is completely cooked.

6. Cook brown rice and add garlic and diced onion for extra flavor. Plate and Enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

Calories 363

Total Fat 6g

Total Carbs 32g

Protein 43g
